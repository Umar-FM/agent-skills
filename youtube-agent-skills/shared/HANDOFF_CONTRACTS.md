# Handoff Contracts

Agents exchange artifacts, not vague prose. Every artifact begins with:

```yaml
artifact_type: <type>
version: 1.0
project_id: <stable-id>
owner: <role>
status: draft|review|locked
updated_at: <ISO-8601>
assumptions: []
risks: []
```

## Channel thesis

Owner: `channel-strategy-architect`

Required fields: target viewer, recurring promise, emotional engine, content pillars, format mix, differentiation, monetization ladder, production constraints, policy boundaries, scorecard, anti-goals.

## Audience evidence packet

Owner: `audience-market-researcher`

Required fields: jobs-to-be-done, pains/desires, language patterns, viewing contexts, alternative channels, content gaps, purchase intent, evidence quality, confidence.

## Concept brief

Owner: `idea-format-developer`

Required fields: one-sentence premise, why-now, viewer, curiosity/stakes, payoff, proof, format, runtime, title seeds, thumbnail seeds, production plan, monetization fit, risks, score.

## Packaging brief

Owner: `packaging-director`

Required fields: target surface, three hypotheses, title set, thumbnail concepts, complementarity logic, promise, proof in video, mobile test, differentiation, A/B plan.

## Research packet

Owner: `research-fact-checker`

Required fields: claim ledger, sources, direct evidence, counterevidence, uncertainty, pronunciation notes, legal/rights notes, visuals that prove each claim, fact-check signoff.

## Script and retention map

Owner: `story-retention-scriptwriter`

Required fields: cold open, click confirmation, central question, stakes, beat table, loop openings/closures, proof moments, visual intent, emotional shifts, payoff, CTA, target runtime.

## Asset manifest

Owner: `faceless-visual-producer`

Required fields: asset ID, script beat, source/creator, license basis, capture date, transformation plan, visual purpose, status, file path, disclosure notes.

## Edit review

Owner: `retention-video-editor`

Required fields: story summary, confusing moments, dead time, dips predicted, proof gaps, audio issues, sponsor impact, runtime changes, unresolved decisions, final acceptance.

## Upload packet

Owner: `publishing-seo-distribution`

Required fields: title, thumbnail, description, chapters, captions, tags only where useful, playlist, end screen, cards, audience setting, disclosures, monetization settings, pinned comment, distribution copy, A/B plan.

## Analytics postmortem

Owner: `analytics-experimentation-scientist`

Required fields: prediction versus actual, metric context, audience/surface mix, packaging result, intro result, retention events, satisfaction proxies, revenue, cost, diagnosis, confidence, next action.

## Revenue packet

Owner: `monetization-revenue-architect`

Required fields: revenue streams, RPM/effective RPM, offer placement, sponsor obligations, affiliate attribution, product fit, contribution margin, next monetization test.

# Ultimate Alex Hormozi Skill Bundle

A modular, agent-ready skill pack for building offers, lead machines, money models, and scaling roadmaps inspired by the public teachings, books, and course/playbook maps associated with Alex Hormozi and Acquisition.com.

This is an educational synthesis and operating system. It is not affiliated with Alex Hormozi, Leila Hormozi, Acquisition.com, Gym Launch, or any related company. It does not reproduce copyrighted book text. It turns public concepts, official course-map structures, and broadly known business frameworks into reusable skills, checklists, drills, and implementation workflows.

## What this bundle does

Use this bundle when the user wants to:

- Create or improve a high-value offer.
- Pick a better niche or customer segment.
- Build lead magnets, outreach systems, content systems, referral systems, affiliate systems, or paid-ad tests.
- Diagnose customer acquisition bottlenecks.
- Design a money model with attraction offers, upsells, downsells, and continuity.
- Improve unit economics: gross profit, CAC, payback period, contribution margin, churn, refund rate, and cash conversion.
- Create a stage-appropriate scaling plan.
- Turn strategy into daily/weekly implementation drills.

## Bundle structure

- `SKILL.md` — core agent behavior, triggers, operating rules, intake, routing, and output contracts.
- `01_source_map_and_architecture.md` — source map, concept inventory, skill architecture, progression model.
- `02_offer_architect_skill.md` — offer creation, market selection, pricing, value equation, bonuses, guarantees, urgency/scarcity, naming.
- `03_lead_generation_machine_skill.md` — lead magnets, first clients, content, cold outreach, paid ads, referrals, employees, agencies, affiliates.
- `04_money_models_skill.md` — CAC, gross profit, payback, offer stacks, attraction offers, upsells, downsells, continuity, cash conversion.
- `05_scaling_roadmap_skill.md` — stage diagnosis, bottleneck selection, systems, team, operations, metrics, 30/60/90-day plans.
- `06_conversion_assets_skill.md` — landing pages, content hooks, outreach messages, sales scripts, proof, objections, follow-up.
- `07_drills_sprints_and_curriculum.md` — drills, sprints, progression levels, training plans, weekly cadence.
- `08_templates_checklists_and_rubrics.md` — reusable worksheets, QA rubrics, scorecards, anti-patterns, red-team prompts.
- `SOURCES.md` — research notes and public-source map.
- `ALL_IN_ONE_ALEX_HORMOZI_SKILL_BUNDLE.md` — consolidated version of all files.
- `templates/` — standalone template files for repeated use.

## Recommended use

Start with `SKILL.md`. The master skill will route the request into one or more modules. For example:

> “Use the Hormozi bundle to audit my offer and rebuild it.”

> “Create a 30-day lead plan for a B2B service business doing $40k/month.”

> “Build a money model for a coaching offer with a paid diagnostic, core program, upsell, downsell, and continuity.”

> “Diagnose our scaling stage and tell us what to fix first.”

## Default output standard

Every output from this bundle should include:

1. **Diagnosis** — current stage, bottleneck, and strongest leverage point.
2. **Artifact** — offer, plan, funnel, script, model, checklist, or roadmap.
3. **Math** — assumptions, variables, and target metrics.
4. **Experiment** — the smallest next test to validate the recommendation.
5. **Scorecard** — what to measure and what would count as success/failure.
6. **Execution plan** — next 7 days, next 30 days, or next 90 days.

## Guardrails

- Do not guarantee financial outcomes.

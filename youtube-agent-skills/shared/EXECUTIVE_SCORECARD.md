# Executive Scorecard

Review weekly for operations and monthly for strategy. Segment by long-form/Shorts, pillar, format, and traffic surface where meaningful.

## Audience and distribution

- Impressions and views by surface
- New and returning viewers
- Unique viewers and subscriber conversion
- Top Suggested neighbors and search terms
- Multi-video and playlist movement

## Packaging

- CTR by Home, Suggested, Search, and Subscriptions
- Native A/B watch-time-share result
- Qualified watch time per impression
- Percentage of uploads requiring repackaging

## Viewing

- 30-second retention
- AVD and APV
- Relative retention for comparable lengths
- Common dip locations: intro, transitions, sponsor, CTA, ending
- Top moments and repeatable viewer-value patterns

## Satisfaction and brand

- Shares and high-quality comments
- Returning-viewer trend
- Corrections, complaints, dislikes where visible, and trust issues
- Audience overlap among pillars and formats

## Revenue

- Ads/Premium, sponsor, affiliate, product, membership, licensing, other
- Revenue per 1,000 views and impressions
- Sponsor effective RPM
- Contribution margin per video and format
- Revenue and contribution per production hour
- Revenue concentration and receivables

## Operations and risk

- Projects by state and time in state
- First-pass acceptance and revision rate
- Cost/hours per video and finished minute
- Rights documentation completeness
- Claims, warnings, demonetization, disclosure, or safety incidents

## Required executive decisions

Every monthly review ends with:

1. Which format/pillar receives more capacity?
2. Which receives less or stops?
3. What audience or package hypothesis will be tested?
4. What production bottleneck will be removed?
5. What revenue layer will be tested or protected?
6. What new risk or policy change requires action?
